Featured Projects

AI-Assisted Design & Post-Layout Verification of a 10-Bit SAR ADC (180nm SCL)

Designed a 10-bit Successive Approximation Register (SAR) ADC in SCL 180nm CMOS technology using Cadence Virtuoso. Applied an XGBoost-based machine learning model to predict initial transistor sizing and analog design parameters, accelerating design-space exploration and reducing manual design iterations. Verified the complete design through schematic and post-layout simulations.

  • Designed a 10-bit SAR ADC in SCL 180nm CMOS technology
  • Applied XGBoost-based ML for transistor sizing and parameter prediction
  • Completed DRC, LVS, PEX and post-layout verification
  • Targeted 9.4-bit ENOB at 10 MS/s with 1.8 V supply
  • Optimized for approximately 1.2 mW power consumption
Cadence Virtuoso SCL 180nm SAR ADC XGBoost Machine Learning DRC/LVS/PEX Post-Layout

3-Stage Active-Inductor CTLE for SerDes (28nm UMC-HPC)

Designed a continuous-time linear equalizer to compensate inter-symbol interference in 28nm high-speed SerDes links. Optimized pole-zero placement for maximal eye opening and verified across process corners.

  • Improved eye opening via targeted pole-zero placement
  • Verified across TT/SS/FF process corners
  • Reduced high-frequency channel loss effects
28nm UMC-HPC Cadence Virtuoso Spectre SerDes

  • Enabled rapid design-space exploration
